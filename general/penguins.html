Penguins Dataset
R

This Penguins dataset was taken from Palmer Penguins library in R.This dataset contains varitey of information about penguins including parameters like bodymass, flipper length and a lot more. I used R libraries like Tidyverse, GGplot2, Palmer Penguins, and R Markdown to visualize the relationship between various parameters in the dataset.

Questions Answered

Does the body mass increase with increase in flipper length ?
After analyzing the dataset by selecting parameters like body mass and flipper length,it is evident that higher the flipper length is proportional to the body mass.
Which type of penguin species have higher body mass ?
Yes, it is clearly seen from the analysis that Gentoo species of penguins have higher body mass.
Did the population of penguins increase over the years, if so then distingush between male and female penguins ?
Yes, the population of penguins increased over the years and the population of female penguins have increased a little more than males penguins.
